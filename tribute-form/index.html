Tribute To Time

The silent companion that shapes every moment of our lives.

Time — it’s invisible, yet it touches everything. It ages mountains, fades memories, and heals wounds. It is the most valuable resource we all share equally, but spend differently. From sunrise to sunset, time teaches us patience and presence. It transforms chaos into order, and ideas into reality. It cannot be stored, paused, or reversed — only respected. This tribute honors Time, the greatest teacher and the most silent witness to all of life’s beauty, tragedy, growth, and change.

Facts About Time

  • Time waits for no one — it moves forward, with or without us.
  • Every second that passes becomes a memory or a missed opportunity.
  • Time is both a healer and a destroyer; it mends wounds but also fades moments.
  • It is the most democratic force — rich or poor, everyone gets 24 hours a day.
  • Time teaches us that change is the only constant in life.
  • You can’t own time, but you can choose how to spend it.
  • Time doesn't stop for anyone — but how we use it can define our legacy.
